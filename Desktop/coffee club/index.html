Our Story

Coffee • Breakfast • Waffles • Korean Food

At Coffee Club, we believe that great coffee is more than just a beverage—it's an experience. Located in Raniban Townplanning, Kathmandu, we serve premium coffee, delicious breakfast, authentic Korean food, and our famous waffles.

We've created a warm, welcoming, pet-friendly space where friends gather, ideas flow, and memories are made. Whether you're starting your day with breakfast, enjoying Korean cuisine, or unwinding with a perfect cup of coffee, we're here to make every moment special. We also offer takeaway and home delivery for your convenience!
